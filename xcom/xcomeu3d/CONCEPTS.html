Conceptual Overview

What the game shold be

The xcomeu3d should inherit most of the gameplay from original X-COM: Ufo Defence(ufo1) game. This means it have to:

  • consist of two parts: economical and tactical
  • economical part is real-time
  • tactical is turn-based, but in future may become real-time (like X-COM: Appocalypse(ufo3))
  • have two sides of gameplay - X-COM and aliens
  • have research tree similar to the ufo1 have.

  • be completely in 3D (more...)
  • have the appearance of items to be different (charactrers, weapons, UI and so on)
